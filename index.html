Behind the scenes of our operations

AI Demand Prediction

Our system predicts exactly which vegetables will be ordered tomorrow, ensuring farmers only harvest what will be sold. This leads to 0% food waste at the farm level.

Satellite Weather Sync

We monitor micro-climate data using satellite feeds. If rain is expected in a specific village, we reroute our supply chain instantly to prevent supply delays to your home.

Automated Smart Routing

Our delivery trucks are guided by a dynamic routing algorithm that avoids traffic and maintains cold-storage temperatures, ensuring crispy delivery by 7 AM.
